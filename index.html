Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a prestigious German automotive brand known for producing luxury vehicles, buses, coaches, and trucks. Founded in 1926, the company's name is a combination of two components: "Mercedes" is derived from a brand name under which Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft (DMG) sold its vehicles, and "Benz" is from Karl Benz, the inventor of the first gasoline-powered car. The brand is renowned for its commitment to craftsmanship, elegance, and cutting-edge technology.

Ferrari
